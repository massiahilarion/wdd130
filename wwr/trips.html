Our Adventures Activities

Available Tour Packages

Scenic River Cruises

Families and beginners looking for a scenic getaway.Drift through lush, forested canyons and splash through mild, beginner-friendly rapids. Unwind on pristine sandy beaches made for relaxing afternoon breaks.

Desert Oasis Camping

Nature lovers seeking a multi-day wilderness immersion.Journey through breathtaking desert rock formations. Camp under the stars after conquering over 60 mild-to-moderate, historic rapids along stunning scenic routes.

Whitewater Thrills

Adrenaline junkies and experienced thrill-seekers.Tackle intense, fast-paced Class III and IV rapids. Navigate tight, high-stakes river bends carved deep into towering, ancient black rock walls.
